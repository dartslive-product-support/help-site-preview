    dl3_004 DARTSLIVE3-LITE FAQ

    This is the main FAQ for DARTSLIVE3 LITE.

    １．No sound from the cabinet

    If the power is cycled on and off too quickly, the safety circuit may activate, preventing sound output.

    In this case, turn off the power, wait at least 5 seconds, then turn it back on.

    ２．Error occurs during target sensor check in Test Mode

    Turn off the power, wait at least 5 seconds, restart the system, then enter Test Mode and check again.

    ３．Sensor errors occur during startup or gameplay

    Wipe any debris off the LITE sensor with a soft cloth. If the issue persists, turn off the power, wait at least 5 seconds, then restart.

    ４．A warning to calibrate the LITE sensor appears every time it starts up

    This warning may appear at startup if you replace the PC or LITE sensor, or change USB ports.

    In such cases, perform target sensor calibration in test mode.

    ５．What features differ between DARTSLIVE3DX/SE and LITE?

    ・DARTSLIVE3-LITE does not have a speed sensor, so content requiring a speed sensor will not function.

    ・Screen resolution differs in some areas between DARTSLIVE3DX/SE and LITE.
