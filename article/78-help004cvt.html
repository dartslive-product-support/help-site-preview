    dl2_004cvt_Camera does not display anything/flickers

    1. Camera does not display anything.

    1) Camera screen is showing white noise.
    Is the camera cable connected?
    Is it plugged in securely?
    Please unplug and plug in the cable again.

    Picture: Camera cable and its connection point Picture: EX-F's capture box

    2) Camera screen goes solid black.
    Is the electricity supplied to the camera and capture box?
    If there is no problem with the power supply,
    there could be a defect in the capture box or cables. Please contact our sales representative.

    3) The camera screen goes black only when online games are played.
    What is the network status?
    If it is too slow, videos may not be displayed properly.
    It is recommended to use as fast a network as possible.

    2. Camera flickers.

    This can happen due to shop's environment.
    The problem may be solved by changing the brightness, angle and/or the type of lighting used in the shop.

    ⇒Using car window's tint film may solve the issue.
    Please use it as required.
